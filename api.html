Overview

One of these days as you keep cracking the encrypted vault, you fall asleep, you dream about the vault you're trying to decipher. All of the sudden, you hear a voice! inside the vault! The voice is programmed to give you everything you need to know about the content of the vault and other connected vaults as well. As long as you authorize yourself to the voice. No more cracking. Just crafted fetching. That is the API dream and you have awaken to use it to your advantage.
